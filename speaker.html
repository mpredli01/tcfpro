4th Annual TCF IT Professional Conference

Speaker Registration

Potential Speakers should submit (by Feb 20, 2009):

  • Abstract (prefer in Microsoft Word format)

  • Biography (prefer in Microsoft Word format)

  • Digital Photo

Each session is 55 minutes long and a projector & screen will be supplied, but no computer.

Please make all submissions to the TCF Professional Conference Chair by sending your abstract, bio, and photo to dsoll@ieee.org.
