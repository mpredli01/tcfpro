16th Annual IEEE IT Professional Conference (ITPC)

Virtual Event

Friday, March 18th, 2022 at 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM

In continuous operation since 1976, the Trenton Computer Festival (TCF) is the nation's longest running personal computer. For the sixteenth year, the TCF is extending its program to provide Information Technology and computer professionals with an additional day of conference. It is intended, in an economical way, to provide attendees with insight and information pertinent to their jobs, and to keep them informed of emerging technologies that could impact their work.

ITPC Event Portal 2022 Program Book

Women in Cybersecurity Roundtable

Cybersecurity continues to be one of the most critical fields in Information Technology, with some of the most interesting, complex and varied career opportunities. Dr. Rebecca Mercuri, who has been performing digital forensic investigations and providing expert witness testimony since 1998, will moderate this panel session that will discuss the many challenges that women face in this rewarding work.
