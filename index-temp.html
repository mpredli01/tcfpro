15th Annual IEEE IT Professional Conference at TCF (2021)

Virtual Event

In continuous operation since 1976, the Trenton Computer Festival (TCF) is the nation's longest running personal computer. For the fifteenth year, the TCF is extending its program to provide Information Technology and computer professionals with an additional day of conference. It is intended, in an economical way, to provide attendees with insight and information pertinent to their jobs, and to keep them informed of emerging technologies that could impact their work.

The IT Professional Conference will be run between 8:30 AM and 5:00 PM on Friday, March 19, 2021 with additional sessions on Saturday, March 20.

IEEE Information Technology Professional Conference

Friday, March 19th, 2021 at 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM

This year's event will be performed virtually via on-line video conferencing

The IT Professional Conference is is co-sponsored by the IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) Computer Society Chapter of Princeton / Central Jersey.

A facilitated networking session will be conducted at lunch to allow speakers and attendees to meet and greet!

Thanks for attending this year's conference! We look forward to seeing you next year!
