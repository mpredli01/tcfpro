Isabel Wagner

Isabel Wagner is an Associate Professor in Computer Science (Cybersecurity) at De Montfort University in Leicester, UK. Dr. Wagner received her Ph.D in engineering and M.Sc. in computer science from the University of Erlangen, Germany, in 2010 and 2005, respectively. She is a Senior Member of the ACM (2017) and IEEE (2018).

Her research interests are privacy and privacy-enhancing technologies, particularly metrics to quantify the effectiveness of privacy protection mechanisms and privacy-enhancing technologies in smart cities, genomics, vehicular networks, and smart grids. She is also interested in web measurement to create transparency for corporate surveillance systems.
